[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The shadows fall, but the fire is wide awake
I trace the outline of the memories we make
Caught in the current of a beautiful mistake
I am drowning without your love, an ache I cannot shake

[Verse 1]
A quiet corner where the amber glow is cast
We leave the ticking clocks to echo in the past
You pull me closer and the room is fading fast
I am holding onto a moment built to last

[Pre-Chorus]
The silence speaks a language only we can know
A sudden rush, a steady heartbeat beating slow
I feel the embers deep inside begin to glow
I never want to turn around and let you go

[Chorus]
I am drowning without your love tonight
Lost in the warmth of a candle's golden light
Every breath you take is pulling me so near
You are the only voice I want to hear

[Post-Chorus]
[Ad-lib] (Drowning, gently going under)
[Echo] (Drowning, caught inside the wonder)
